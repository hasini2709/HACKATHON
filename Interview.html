GOVERNMENT JOBS

Defence

Defence jobs in India are positions in the military or paramilitary forces. Serving in the army, navy, air force, or other relevant institutions is part of a defence job.

SSC

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) is an organisation under the Government of India to recruit staff for various posts in the various ministries and departments of the government of India and in subordinate offices and agencies

UPSC

Union Public Service Commission is the premier recruiting agency for the Government of India. UPSC is responsible for recruiting candidates for All India Services, Central Services, and Cadres, as well as the Armed Forces of the Union of India.

Jobs Under Indian Defence Exams

Explore different Indian defence exams, jobs, and their respective paper models.

National Defence Academy (NDA) / Naval Academy (NA)

The NDA/NA exam is conducted by UPSC for recruitment to the Army, Navy, and Air Force wings of the NDA and NA.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 16.5 to 19.5 years
  • Educational Qualification: 10+2 for Army, Physics and Maths in 10+2 for Navy and Air Force

Paper Model:

  • Mathematics: 300 marks, Objective type, 2.5 hours
  • General Ability Test (GAT): 600 marks, Objective type, 2.5 hours

Combined Defence Services (CDS)

The CDS exam is also conducted by UPSC for recruitment to the Indian Military Academy (IMA), Indian Naval Academy (INA), Air Force Academy (AFA), and Officers' Training Academy (OTA).

Eligibility:

  • Age: 19 to 24 years
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation or equivalent

Paper Model:

  • IMA, INA, AFA:
    • English: 100 marks, Objective type, 2 hours
    • General Knowledge: 100 marks, Objective type, 2 hours
    • Elementary Mathematics: 100 marks, Objective type, 2 hours
  • OTA:
    • English: 100 marks, Objective type, 2 hours
    • General Knowledge: 100 marks, Objective type, 2 hours

Air Force Common Admission Test (AFCAT)

AFCAT is conducted by the Indian Air Force for recruitment in Flying Branch, Ground Duty (Technical and Non-Technical Branches).

Eligibility:

  • Age: 20 to 24 years for Flying Branch, 20 to 26 years for Ground Duty
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation or Engineering degree

Paper Model:

  • AFCAT Paper: 100 questions, 300 marks, Objective type, 2 hours
  • EKT (Engineering Knowledge Test): 50 questions, 150 marks, Objective type, 45 minutes (For technical candidates)

Indian Coast Guard Assistant Commandant

The exam is conducted for recruitment of officers in General Duty, Commercial Pilot License (CPL), and Technical Branches.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 21 to 25 years
  • Educational Qualification: 10+2 with Physics and Maths, or Engineering degree

Paper Model:

  • Preliminary Exam: Objective type, covering Mental Ability, Reasoning, General Awareness
  • Final Selection: Interview, Medical Test, and Physical Fitness Test

Territorial Army Officer

The Territorial Army exam is conducted for recruitment of part-time officers who can join the army voluntarily.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 18 to 42 years
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation in any discipline

Paper Model:

  • Paper 1: Reasoning and Elementary Mathematics (100 marks, Objective type)
  • Paper 2: General Knowledge and English (100 marks, Objective type)

Indian Navy Sailor Entry (SSR/AA)

SSR (Senior Secondary Recruit) and AA (Artificer Apprentice) are entry-level exams for recruitment in Indian Navy.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 17 to 21 years
  • Educational Qualification: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Maths

Paper Model:

  • SSR/AA Paper: 100 questions, 1 hour, Objective type covering Science, Mathematics, General Knowledge

SSC Combined Graduate Level (CGL) Exam

SSC CGL is conducted for recruitment in Group B and Group C posts in various ministries, departments, and organizations of the Government of India.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 18 to 32 years (varies for different posts)
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation in any discipline

Paper Model:

  • Tier 1 (Objective): General Intelligence and Reasoning, General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude, English Comprehension (200 marks, 60 minutes)
  • Tier 2 (Objective): Quantitative Abilities (200 marks), English Language (200 marks), Statistics (200 marks), General Studies (200 marks)
  • Tier 3 (Descriptive): Essay/Precis/Letter Writing (100 marks, 60 minutes)
  • Tier 4: Data Entry Skill Test (DEST) / Computer Proficiency Test (CPT)

SSC Combined Higher Secondary Level (CHSL) Exam

SSC CHSL is conducted for recruitment of Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), Postal Assistant (PA), Sorting Assistant (SA), and Data Entry Operator (DEO).

Eligibility:

  • Age: 18 to 27 years
  • Educational Qualification: 10+2 or equivalent

Paper Model:

  • Tier 1 (Objective): General Intelligence, English Language, Quantitative Aptitude, General Awareness (200 marks, 60 minutes)
  • Tier 2 (Descriptive): Essay/Letter/Application Writing (100 marks, 60 minutes)
  • Tier 3: Typing Test/Skill Test (for Data Entry Operators)

SSC Multitasking Staff (MTS) Exam

SSC MTS is conducted for recruitment of Multitasking Staff in various central government ministries and departments.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 18 to 25 years
  • Educational Qualification: Matriculation/10th pass

Paper Model:

  • Tier 1 (Objective): General Intelligence and Reasoning, Numerical Aptitude, General English, General Awareness (100 marks, 90 minutes)
  • Tier 2 (Descriptive): Short Essay/Letter Writing (50 marks, 30 minutes)

SSC Junior Engineer (JE) Exam

SSC JE is conducted for recruitment of Junior Engineers in Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Quantity Surveying & Contracts.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 18 to 32 years (varies by post)
  • Educational Qualification: Diploma/Degree in Engineering

Paper Model:

  • Paper 1 (Objective): General Intelligence and Reasoning, General Awareness, Engineering Subject (200 marks, 2 hours)
  • Paper 2 (Descriptive): Engineering Subject (300 marks, 2 hours)

SSC General Duty (GD) Constable Exam

SSC GD is conducted for recruitment of constables in CAPFs (Central Armed Police Forces), NIA (National Investigation Agency), SSF (Secretariat Security Force), and Rifleman (Assam Rifles).

Eligibility:

  • Age: 18 to 23 years
  • Educational Qualification: Matriculation/10th pass

Paper Model:

  • Tier 1 (Objective): General Intelligence and Reasoning, General Knowledge, Numerical Ability, English/Hindi (100 marks, 90 minutes)
  • Physical Test: Physical Efficiency Test (PET) and Physical Standard Test (PST)

UPSC Exams Information

Explore different UPSC exams, jobs, and their respective paper models.

UPSC Civil Services Exam (CSE)

UPSC CSE is conducted for recruitment to IAS, IPS, IFS, and other central services. It is one of the most prestigious exams in India.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 21 to 32 years
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation in any discipline

Paper Model:

  • Preliminary Exam:
    • General Studies Paper 1 (200 marks, 2 hours)
    • General Studies Paper 2 (CSAT) (200 marks, 2 hours, qualifying)
  • Main Exam:
    • Essay (250 marks)
    • General Studies I (250 marks)
    • General Studies II (250 marks)
    • General Studies III (250 marks)
    • General Studies IV (250 marks)
    • Optional Subject Paper I (250 marks)
    • Optional Subject Paper II (250 marks)
  • Personality Test (Interview): 275 marks

UPSC Indian Engineering Services (IES/ESE)

UPSC IES exam is conducted for recruitment of engineers in various government departments such as Railways, Central Water Engineering, Defense Services, etc.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 21 to 30 years
  • Educational Qualification: Engineering degree

Paper Model:

  • Preliminary Exam:
    • General Studies and Engineering Aptitude (200 marks, 2 hours)
    • Engineering Discipline Paper (300 marks, 3 hours)
  • Main Exam:
    • Engineering Discipline Paper I (300 marks)
    • Engineering Discipline Paper II (300 marks)
  • Personality Test: 200 marks

UPSC Combined Defence Services (CDS) Exam

UPSC CDS is conducted for recruitment in the Indian Military Academy (IMA), Indian Naval Academy (INA), Air Force Academy (AFA), and Officers' Training Academy (OTA).

Eligibility:

  • Age: 19 to 25 years
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation (Engineering for INA, Physics/Math for AFA)

Paper Model:

  • IMA, INA, AFA:
    • English (100 marks, 2 hours)
    • General Knowledge (100 marks, 2 hours)
    • Mathematics (100 marks, 2 hours)
  • OTA:
    • English (100 marks, 2 hours)
    • General Knowledge (100 marks, 2 hours)
  • SSB Interview: 300 marks for IMA, INA, AFA, and 200 marks for OTA

UPSC Indian Forest Services (IFS) Exam

UPSC IFS exam is conducted for recruitment of officers in the Indian Forest Services to manage forest resources and wildlife.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 21 to 32 years
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation in science/engineering

Paper Model:

  • Preliminary Exam: Same as Civil Services Prelims
  • Main Exam:
    • General English (300 marks)
    • General Knowledge (300 marks)
    • Optional Subject Paper I (200 marks)
    • Optional Subject Paper II (200 marks)
  • Personality Test: 300 marks

UPSC Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF) Exam

UPSC CAPF exam is conducted for recruitment of Assistant Commandants in Central Armed Police Forces such as BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, and SSB.

Eligibility:

  • Age: 20 to 25 years
  • Educational Qualification: Graduation in any discipline

Paper Model:

  • Paper 1: General Ability and Intelligence (250 marks, 2 hours)
  • Paper 2: General Studies, Essay, and Comprehension (200 marks, 3 hours)
  • Physical and Medical Test:
  • Interview/Personality Test: 150 marks
